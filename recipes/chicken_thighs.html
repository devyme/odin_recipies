Easy Baked Chicken Thighs Recipe.

Let's make some great chicken thighs!

These easy baked chicken thighs require minimum preparation; I love this quick recipe because I'm a college student! Feel free to season the chicken with your favorite spices. I like to use cayenne powder and black pepper.

Ingredient:

  • 4 Chicken thighs
  • 4 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 teaspoons onion flakes

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C (375 degrees F)
  2. Place chicken thighs in a baking dish; season both sides with garlic powder and onion flakes.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer poink at the bone and juices run clearm about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of thigh, near the bone, should read 74 degrees C (165 degrees F).
